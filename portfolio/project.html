DRIVER DROWSINESS DETECTION


Welcome to my driver drowsiness detection project, aimed at enhancing road safety through real-time monitoring of driver alertness.
This project showcases:
Computer Vision Integration: computer vision algorithms (such as facial landmark detection and eye tracking) to analyze driver behavior and detect signs of drowsiness or fatigue.
Sensor Integration: Utilizing sensors (like IR sensors or cameras) to monitor physiological signals (e.g., eye closure duration, head position) indicative of drowsiness, ensuring accurate detection.
Real-time Alerts: Providing timely alerts to drivers through auditory or visual cues when drowsiness is detected, promoting immediate corrective action and preventing potential accidents.
Data Logging and Analysis: Logging data on driver behavior and fatigue patterns over time, enabling insights into driving habits and supporting long-term safety improvements.
